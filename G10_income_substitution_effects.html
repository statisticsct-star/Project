The bar chart uses the official annual tax-cut figures published by the Treasury fact sheet for selected incomes.
Policy facts used in this case
  • Rates from 1 July 2024: 19% fell to 16%; 32.5% fell to 30%; the 37% threshold rose to $135,000; the 45% threshold rose to $190,000.
  • Coverage: 13.6 million taxpayers received a cut from 2024–25 onward.
  • Examples: the official annual tax cut is $804 at $45,000, $1,554 at $75,000, $2,179 at $100,000, and $3,729 at $150,000.
  • Labour-supply estimate: Treasury estimated the redesign would lift labour supply by about 930,000 hours per week, or 0.25%.
  • Labour-market context in July 2024: ABS reported a 67.1% participation rate and 1,961 million seasonally adjusted monthly hours worked.
